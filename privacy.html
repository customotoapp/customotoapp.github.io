1. Introduction

CustoMoto! ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by CUSTOMOTO COMPUTER PROGRAMMING SERVICES, a sole proprietorship registered with the Department of Trade and Industry of the Philippines (DTI Business Name No. 8091235), Region IV-A (CALABARZON), Philippines.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the CustoMoto! mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012 ("DPA"), and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Policy.

2. Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Password — stored in encrypted form via Firebase Authentication; we never see your plain-text password
  • Country and region — collected during onboarding to personalise your experience
  • Motorcycle details — make, model, year, and customisation preferences you enter or save
  • Profile information — display name and other optional fields you choose to provide

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — features used, screens visited, and interactions within the App
  • Log data — IP address, access times, and crash reports
  • Firebase Analytics data — anonymised usage patterns to improve the App

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In — if you use Google OAuth to log in, we receive your name and email address from Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide, operate, and improve the App and its features
  • To personalise your experience, including showing relevant motorcycle parts and customisation options
  • To process in-app purchases (Moto Coins) and manage your subscription status
  • To send you important notices, updates, and support communications
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
  • To comply with legal obligations under Philippine law
  • To analyse usage trends and improve our services

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent — you have given us clear consent to process your data for specific purposes
  • Contract — processing is necessary to perform the App services you have requested
  • Legitimate interests — to detect fraud, improve our services, and ensure App security
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable Philippine laws and regulations

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and hosting (Google LLC, USA). Data is stored in the asia-southeast1 (Singapore) region.
  • Google Play Billing / Apple StoreKit — in-app purchase processing
  • PayMongo / Xendit — local Philippine payment processing for GCash and Maya payments
  • Cloudinary — image hosting and processing for shared build images

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in the Philippines, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CustoMoto!, our users, or the public.

5.3 Business Transfer

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice in the App before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained for the duration of your account and for 90 days after deletion
  • Build and customisation data — retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Transaction records — retained for 5 years as required by Philippine tax and accounting regulations
  • Log data — retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us at wrathfulrealm@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012

  • Right to be informed — to know what personal data we collect and how we use it
  • Right to access — to request a copy of your personal data that we hold
  • Right to rectification — to have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected
  • Right to erasure or blocking — to have your data deleted or blocked under certain circumstances
  • Right to object — to object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to lodge a complaint — to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at wrathfulrealm@gmail.com. We will respond within 15 working days.

8. Data Security

  • Firebase Authentication with encrypted password storage
  • Firestore Security Rules that restrict each user to their own data only
  • Server-side validation for all sensitive operations including payments and subscriptions
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse

9. Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at wrathfulrealm@gmail.com and we will delete such information promptly.
